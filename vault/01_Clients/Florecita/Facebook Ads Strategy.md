## Target Audience
- Phoenix metro residents
- Foodies / dining out enthusiasts
- Ages 25-55
- Interest in Mexican cuisine, fine dining, date nights

## Campaign Objective
- Local awareness + foot traffic (primary)
- Online reservations / orders (secondary)

## Funnel Structure
- **Top of Funnel:** Mouth-watering food imagery/video → restaurant page
- **Middle of Funnel:** Retarget with specials, events, reviews
- **Bottom of Funnel:** Direct reservation/order CTA to warm audiences

## Creative Angles
- Food photography / video (hero dishes)
- Behind-the-scenes kitchen content
- "Your new favorite spot in Phoenix"
- Seasonal specials and events
- Date night positioning
- Happy hour / deal-driven

## Retargeting Opportunities
- Website visitors
- Instagram engagers (critical for restaurants)
- People who viewed menu page but didn't reserve
- Previous customers (email list)
- Lookalike from existing customers

## Notes
- Cross-reference with Google Ads performance to avoid audience overlap
- Instagram likely more important than Facebook for this client
