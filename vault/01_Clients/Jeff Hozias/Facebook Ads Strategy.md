## Funnel Structure
- **Top of Funnel:** Market update content, home valuation offers, neighborhood guides
- **Middle of Funnel:** Retarget with testimonials, sold properties, market insights
- **Bottom of Funnel:** Direct "Get your free home valuation" or "See new listings" CTA

## Creative Angles
- "What's your home worth?" (seller lead gen)
- "Just listed" / "Just sold" social proof
- Market update / data-driven content
- Neighborhood spotlight series
- First-time buyer education
- "Thinking about selling? Here's what your neighbors got"

## Retargeting Opportunities
- Website visitors (property page viewers especially)
- Video viewers (market update videos)
- Lead form openers who didn't submit
- Past client email list → lookalike
- Engaged with Facebook/Instagram page

## Notes

- This limits targeting: no age, gender, zip code targeting
- Focus on interest/behavior targeting within housing restrictions
