# Buzz Bull — April 2026 Meta Campaign Build

#ads #meta #buzzbull #leadgen

**Avatar:** Owner-operators running home services, contractors, HVAC, roofing, landscaping, med spas, local SMBs doing $500K–$5M. They close jobs themselves, hate marketing jargon, and have been burned by at least one agency.

**Core Offer:** Done-for-you lead system — Ads + SEO + CRM follow-up — built to book jobs on the calendar, not impressions on a dashboard. Flat monthly. No setup fees. 30-day performance guarantee.

## STEP 1 — 5 HIGH-CONVERTING ANGLES

### Angle 1 — Pain-Driven ("The Phone Stopped Ringing")
**Thesis:** Owner is watching the pipeline dry up and blaming the economy. It's not the economy. It's the funnel.
**Emotional trigger:** Fear, frustration, sleep loss.
**Proof shape:** Before/after booked-jobs calendar screenshot.

### Angle 2 — ROI-Driven ("$4,200 In, $38K Out")
**Thesis:** Marketing isn't an expense, it's a vending machine — put $1 in, pull $6 out, or we're fired.
**Emotional trigger:** Greed, certainty, math.
**Proof shape:** Spend vs revenue line chart, client names, industries.

### Angle 3 — Leads Leaking ("Your Bucket Has Holes")
**Thesis:** You don't have a lead problem. You have a leak problem. 40% of leads never get a call back inside 5 minutes, so they book your competitor.
**Emotional trigger:** Loss aversion — they're already paying for these leads.
**Proof shape:** Diagram of the leaky funnel, CRM speed-to-lead stat.

### Angle 4 — Authority ("We Replace 7 Companies")
**Thesis:** Stop duct-taping 7 vendors (ads guy, SEO guy, web guy, CRM guy, review guy, email guy, reporting guy). One team. One invoice. One throat to choke.
**Emotional trigger:** Overwhelm → relief.
**Proof shape:** 7 logos crossed out → one Buzz Bull logo.

### Angle 5 — Simple Growth Hook ("Add $40K/Month In 60 Days")
**Thesis:** One sentence, one number, one timeline. No fluff. Book a call, see the plan, or don't.
**Emotional trigger:** Clarity, urgency.
**Proof shape:** Raw screenshot of client revenue jump.

## STEP 5 — PERFORMANCE LOGIC

### Success Targets (First 30 Days)
| Metric | Target | Great |
|---|---|---|
| CTR (link) | ≥ 1.5% | ≥ 2.5% |
| CPC | ≤ $2.50 | ≤ $1.50 |
| CPL (form) | ≤ $60 | ≤ $35 |
| CPBC (booked call) | ≤ $180 | ≤ $110 |
| Show rate | ≥ 65% | ≥ 80% |
| Close rate call→client | ≥ 20% | ≥ 30% |
| CAC payback | ≤ 45 days | ≤ 20 days |

### Kill Criteria (Ad Level)
- Spent $50, 0 leads → kill
- CPL > 2x target after $100 spend → kill
- CTR < 0.8% after 10K impressions → kill
- Frequency > 3.5 on cold audience → refresh creative

### Kill Criteria (Ad Set Level)
- $300 spent, 0 booked calls → pause, diagnose
- CPBC > 2x target for 5 consecutive days → pause

### Scaling Criteria
- CPL ≤ target for 3 consecutive days → +20% budget
- CPBC ≤ target AND show rate ≥ 65% → duplicate ad set at +50% budget
- Winning ad hits 2x target ROAS → promote to new lookalike stack (1% LAL of booked-call audience)
- Never scale more than +20% in 24h on a single ad set — resets learning

### Creative Refresh Cadence
- New creative batch every 14 days
- Rotate in top performer variations (same angle, new hook, new image)
- Retire any ad with frequency > 4 on cold
