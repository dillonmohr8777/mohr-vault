# Agent Memory

## Brand Voice
- Operator-to-operator, blunt, anti-agency. Talks to owners who close jobs themselves.
- Gritty / blue-collar direct response (Mike Rowe meets Hormozi), NOT corporate SaaS.
- No marketing jargon. Plain numbers, plain promises. Confidence through restraint.

## Offers to Push
- Done-for-you lead system: Ads + SEO + CRM follow-up, built to book jobs on the calendar.
- Flat monthly, no setup fees, 30-day performance guarantee ("don't book you more jobs than you paid us → no month 2").
- "Add $40K/month in 60 days, or you don't pay" framing.
- Positioned as replacing 7 vendors with one team / one invoice.

## Winning Angles
- Pain-driven — "The Phone Stopped Ringing" (it's not the economy, it's the funnel).
- ROI-driven — "$4,200 In, $38,140 Out" (9x, tracked click → call → contract → deposit).
- Leaks — "Your Bucket Has Holes" (40% of leads never get a 5-min callback).
- Authority — "Fire 7 Vendors, Hire 1 Team."
- Simple growth — "+$40K/Month In 60 Days. Or You Don't Pay."
- Note: angles are built/launched, not yet validated by results.

## Landing Page Notes
- One LP per angle, same core offer, different top-of-page narrative.
- Calendly embed instead of a form (removes a step); above-fold + after-every-section CTAs.
- Exit-intent popup offering "90-day revenue playbook" for email capture → nurture.
- Mobile: sticky bottom CTA bar.

## Ad Notes
- Campaign: `BB_LeadGen_2026-04_Conversions`, objective Leads/Conversions, optimize `Lead` + `Schedule` events.
- CBO $150/day to start; scale +20% every 3 days on ROAS >2x. US only, age 30–60, Advantage+ placements (exclude Audience Network), 7-day click / 1-day view.
- 3 ad sets: Broad SMB (50%), Interest Stack / "Owner Intent" (30%), Retargeting/Warm (20%).
- All 5 angle ads run in all ad sets; launch 5 ads/ad set, kill bottom 2 after $50 spend.
- First-30-day targets: CPL ≤ $60, CPBC ≤ $180, link CTR ≥ 1.5%, show rate ≥ 65%, close rate ≥ 20%.
- Kill rules: $50 spend + 0 leads → kill ad; CTR <0.8% after 10K impr → kill; frequency >3.5 cold → refresh.
- Creative refresh every 14 days. UTM: `fb_{{angle}}_{{adset}}_{{ad}}`.

## Things to Avoid
- No "brand awareness" / "impressions" pitches — sell booked jobs and revenue.
- No corporate SaaS aesthetic or marketing jargon.
