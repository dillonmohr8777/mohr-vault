# Retargeting Ad Ideas

> Ad concepts for warm audiences across all clients.

## Universal Retargeting Ads
- Testimonial / review showcase
- "Still thinking about it?" reminder
- FAQ objection handler
- Limited time urgency
- Social proof (X people signed up this month)

## By Funnel Stage
### Visited site, didn't convert
- Remind them of the offer
- Add social proof
- Address common objections

### Engaged with content
- Deeper value proposition
- Case study / results
- Direct CTA

### Started form but didn't complete
- "You're almost there"
- Simplify the ask
- Add urgency
