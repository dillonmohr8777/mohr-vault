Timecodes are `M:SS.f` (f = frame, 0–29). Each scene lists **VISUAL / MOTION**, **ON‑SCREEN TEXT**, **LOGO**, **SOUND** (full cues in `05`).

## Editor's pacing notes
- **Cut on the downbeat, not every beat.** At ~120 BPM a bar = 2.0s; each scene above is a clean multiple of bars. Land each headline on a "1," build the hero graphic across the next 2–4 bars, hold.
- **Text cadence:** one idea per card; headline holds ≥1.5s before support text. Never make the viewer read and track motion at once — hold the visual still while text lands.
- **Stagger entrances:** eyebrow → headline → graphic, ~6–10 frames apart, all eased (ease‑out in, faster ease‑in out).
- **The two "impact" moments** that must hit hardest: the logo land (0:03.18) and the CTA logo stinger (0:55.0). Reserve the biggest SFX + a beat of silence before each.
