# 01 — Creative Brief

## The one-line concept
In 2026, businesses get *found* when their content is both **beautiful** and **built for AI**. Momentum 360 makes immersive visual content (360° tours, Matterport, photography, HD video, 3D renders) that's engineered to be **seen, cited, and chosen** in both Google and AI search.

## Title
- **Primary:** *Momentum 360: Immersive Content + AI Visibility That Wins*
- Alt 1: *How Businesses Are Getting Found in the AI Era*
- Alt 2: *The New Standard for Visibility in 2026*

## Audience
Owners & marketing leaders at: **real estate**, SMBs, **restaurants**, **retail**, **healthcare practices**, and **professional services**. They're time‑poor, results‑driven, and increasingly aware that "doing SEO" isn't enough anymore. The video must feel premium, current, and a little urgent — *the rules changed; here's how you win.*

## Tone
Energetic, confident, modern, premium. Benefit‑driven and a touch urgent — never cheesy or corporate‑stocky. Matches Momentum 360's own voice ("Elevate," "Boost," "Get found," "take your business to the next level").

## Why no voiceover works here
The story is carried entirely by **edit + kinetic text + motion + music**. This is deliberate: 85%+ of social video is watched muted, and a VO‑free piece drops cleanly into Reels/Shorts/LinkedIn and a website hero loop. Every meaning is delivered on‑screen, so the piece reads fully even on mute.

## Reference video teardown (`lsahighlight720p.mp4`)
A frame‑by‑frame study so the new piece inherits the exact format. *The reference is a Momentum Digital "Local Service Ads + SEO for Realtors" piece — same brand family, same medallion logo.* We keep the skeleton and upgrade the content.

**Technical facts (measured):** 60.05s · **720×1280, 9:16 vertical** · 30fps · H.264 · **audio track is silent (−91 dB)** — the reference carries no music; it's a pure visual/kinetic piece. (We *add* music + light SFX — no voice.)

### The repeating "section unit" (this is the template)
Every middle section of the reference is built from the same three stacked parts. **Reuse this unit for every new scene:**
1. **Eyebrow kicker** — tiny UPPERCASE, letter‑spaced label with a leading bullet/diamond, color‑coded (e.g. `◆ HIGH-INTENT SEARCH`). Sits top‑center.
2. **Two‑tone headline** — bold rounded sans; most words white, **one key word in the section accent color** (e.g. *They sit **above** it all*).
3. **Hero motion graphic** — the proof object: a typing search bar, a 3D stack of ranking cards, a glossy 3D check button, a rising 3D bar chart, or numbered "playbook" pills. Builds in *after* the headline lands.

### Signature visual DNA to replicate
- **Background:** near‑black navy "space," soft **out‑of‑focus color bokeh** blobs drifting (it shifts blue/green/orange/red), faint **starfield dots**, and thin **geometric line‑art** (triangles, open arrowheads) floating in the corners. Subtle, always moving, never busy.
- **Logo medallion:** a circular **coin/portal** with concentric thin rings + a soft outer halo; interior is a **bright blue cloudy‑sky photo texture**; a hand‑drawn **white cursive lowercase "m"** sits in the center. It reveals by **scaling up with a slight 3D Y‑axis tilt/rotation**, a light‑sweep across the m, and a glow bloom.
- **Type:** heavy **rounded geometric sans** (Poppins/Montserrat‑family feel), tight leading, sentence case headlines, the one accent word carrying the color.
- **Motion:** everything **slides up + fades** on eased curves; cards stack in **3D perspective**; objects are **glossy 3D with floor reflections**; numbers **count up**. Calm, premium easing — nothing snaps harshly except deliberate impacts.
- **Pacing:** ~**8–10s per section**; headline + eyebrow land first (~0.5‑1s), then the hero graphic builds over the next few seconds and holds.

## What we change (the upgrade)
- **Color:** consolidate the reference's per‑section rainbow into a disciplined **blue + yellow** system (see `03`). Blue = primary/structure/most accent words; **Yellow = the payoff** (key numbers, the merge, the CTA).
- **Content:** swap "LSA + SEO for realtors" for **immersive visual content + AEO/GEO** for *all* of Momentum 360's verticals.
- **Sound:** add a music bed + light SFX (no voice) — the reference was silent.
- **Logo:** same medallion treatment, used at open, end, and as a small persistent corner bug.
