# 06 — Thumbnail (Cover) & End-Card

Both match the reference aesthetic: dark space backdrop, glowing medallion, two‑tone bold type, blue + yellow. High contrast = high CTR and screenshot value.

---

## 1. Thumbnail / cover frame

**Goal:** stop the scroll in <1s, readable on a phone, ≤3 words of huge text, one face or one striking object.

### Layout (vertical 1080×1920 cover; also export 16:9 1280×720)
- **Background:** the space gradient + a strong blue/yellow bokeh glow behind the focal point.
- **Focal object (pick one):**
  - **A) Split "before/after":** left = a flat, lifeless listing/search result; right = a glowing 360° tour + an AI answer citing the business. Diagonal blue→yellow seam. *(Before/after framing tests ~35% higher CTR.)*
  - **B) Hero object:** the rotating 360° tour sphere with an AI‑answer panel citing it, medallion glowing above.
  - **C) Human + product:** a confident business owner reacting to their listing ranking #1 (faces lift CTR ~20–35%).
- **Text (huge, Poppins 800):** `GET FOUND` **[W]** / `IN THE AI ERA` **[Y]** — or `SEEN. CITED.` **[W]** / `CHOSEN.` **[Y]**
- **Medallion:** top‑center or upper‑corner, glowing.
- **Keep all text/logo out of the lower‑right** (duration stamp) and within title‑safe.

### Specs
- YouTube/landscape cover: **1280×720**, <2MB, JPG/PNG.
- Vertical cover (Reels/Shorts/TikTok): **1080×1920**, key elements in the center, clear of bottom UI + right action rail.
- Produce **2–3 variants** (A/B/C above) to test.
