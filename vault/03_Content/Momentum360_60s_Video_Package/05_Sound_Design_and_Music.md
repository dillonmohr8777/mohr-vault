# 05 — Sound Design & Music (NO voiceover)

The reference is silent — we add a music bed + light SFX only. **No voice, no lyrics** (lyrics compete with kinetic text). Music is the backbone: **pick the track first, then cut to it.**

---

## 1. Music brief
- **Genre:** modern corporate‑electronic / "uplifting tech" — clean, driving, premium. Think product‑launch energy, not stocky.
- **Tempo:** **118–124 BPM** (target ~120 → 1 bar = 2.0s, which the scene grid is built on).
- **Structure (must have a build):** soft intro → groove establishes → steady build through the middle → **peak at the proof section** → brief drop/breath → **resolve on the CTA**.
- **Instrumentation:** pulsing synth bass, light arps, soft plucks, a four‑on‑the‑floor or muted kick groove, airy pads. Avoid vocal chops with words.
- **No lyrics.** Mood: confident, forward, optimistic.

## 3. Mix & delivery rules
- **It must read fully muted** (no‑VO + captions test) — the SFX/music *enhance* but never *carry* meaning.
- Duck SFX so they sit under the music; reserve headroom for the two impacts (0:03.18, 0:55.0).
- Master loudness target ~**−14 LUFS** integrated (social‑safe), true‑peak ≤ −1 dBTP.
- Deliver: full mix (music+SFX), plus **music‑only** and **SFX‑only** stems for flexibility.
