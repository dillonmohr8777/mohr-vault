# Lead Form Ad Copy Ideas

> Copy templates for Meta Lead Form campaigns.

## Structure
- Hook (1 line)
- Problem agitation (1-2 lines)
- Solution (1-2 lines)
- CTA (1 line)

## Templates

### Template 1: Question -> Solution
"[Question about pain point]?
Most [audience] struggle with [problem] because [reason].
We help [audience] [achieve result] through [method].
[CTA] - it takes 30 seconds."

### Template 2: Stat -> Offer
"[Surprising statistic].
That means [implication for audience].
[Brand] helps you [solution] - and it starts with [free offer].
[CTA] to get started."

### Template 3: Social Proof -> CTA
"[X] [audience type] already chose [brand].
Here's why: [key benefit 1], [key benefit 2], [key benefit 3].
[CTA] and see for yourself."
