# Facebook Ads Hook Library

> Proven hooks and opening lines for Meta ads. Organized by type.

## Question Hooks
- "Are you still [pain point]?"
- "What if you could [desired outcome] in [timeframe]?"
- "Did you know [surprising stat]?"
- "Ready to stop [frustration]?"

## Statement Hooks
- "This changed everything for [audience]."
- "Most [audience] get this wrong."
- "Here's why your [thing] isn't working."
- "Stop doing [common mistake]."

## Story Hooks
- "Last year we helped [client type] go from [before] to [after]."
- "When [client] came to us, they were [problem]. Now they're [result]."
- "I used to [relatable problem]. Then I discovered [solution]."

## Urgency Hooks
- "Only [X] spots left."
- "This offer ends [date]."
- "Don't wait until [consequence]."

## Social Proof Hooks
- "[X] businesses already use this."
- "See why [X] people chose [brand]."
- "Rated [X] stars by [Y] customers."
