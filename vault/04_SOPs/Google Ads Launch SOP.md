# SOP: Google Ads Campaign Launch

## When to Use
When launching a new Google Ads account or campaign for any client.

## Pre-Launch Checklist
- [ ] Conversion tracking live (Google Ads tag or GA4 import)
- [ ] Conversion actions defined (Lead form, Call, Purchase) with correct values and counting (one per click for leads)
- [ ] Call tracking set up (Google forwarding numbers or call extension + call-from-ads conversion)
- [ ] UTM parameters on all final URLs
- [ ] Landing page tested: loads under 3s, mobile-friendly, CTA above the fold
- [ ] Budget confirmed with client (daily = monthly / 30.4)
- [ ] Billing set up and verified (no payment holds)
- [ ] Conversion tracking validated with a real test click before spend

## Campaign Type (by client goal)
- **Search** — high-intent lead gen, defined keywords, tight budget control. Default for most service clients.
- **Performance Max** — only with strong conversion data and good asset volume; use after Search proves out, not cold.
- **Local / LSA** — local service businesses (HVAC, plumbing, legal). LSA = pay-per-lead, Google Guaranteed; run alongside Search.

## Account & Campaign Structure
- One campaign per service line or goal; tight ad groups (3-5 keywords each, single theme).
- Naming: `[Client] - [Type] - [Service/Goal] - [Geo]`
- Ad group naming: `[Service] - [Match Theme]`

## Keywords & Negatives
- Start with Phrase and Exact match. Avoid broad match on cold accounts until conversion data exists.
- Build initial negative keyword list before launch: `free`, `jobs`, `careers`, `salary`, `cheap`, `DIY`, `reviews`, `complaints`, competitor brand names, and irrelevant adjacent terms.
- Attach negatives at campaign level; create a shared negative list for cross-campaign terms.

## Ad Creation (RSA Best Practices)
- [ ] 15 headlines, 4 descriptions per RSA (max the asset slots)
- [ ] Include keyword in 2-3 headlines, benefits/offer/CTA in others
- [ ] Pin sparingly — only brand name or required legal/disclaimer; over-pinning kills performance
- [ ] Ad Strength at "Good" or "Excellent" before publishing
- [ ] 2 RSAs per ad group

## Assets (Extensions)
- [ ] Sitelinks (min 4)
- [ ] Callouts (min 4)
- [ ] Structured snippets (1-2 categories)
- [ ] Call asset + location asset where relevant

## Bidding (Cold Accounts)
- Start on **Maximize Conversions** if tracking is solid, or **Manual CPC** if you want tighter control while validating.
- Do NOT start on tCPA/tROAS — not enough data.
- Switch to **tCPA** once campaign has ~15-30 conversions in 30 days; **tROAS** only with value tracking and ~30+ conversions.

## Post-Launch (First 72 Hours)
- [ ] Confirm ads approved and serving (check Ad status)
- [ ] Confirm conversion tracking firing (real conversion or test)
- [ ] Check search terms for early garbage; add obvious negatives
- [ ] Confirm spend is pacing (not capped or stalled)
- [ ] No disapprovals or policy flags

## Notes
- Do NOT touch bids, budgets, or targeting during the learning period (first ~1-2 weeks / until learning completes). Adjustments reset learning.
- Fix disapproved ads by editing copy; if a whole ad needs rebuild, create new rather than churning the same one.
