# SOP: Competitor Depth Research

## When to Use
Per competitor, when building or refreshing a client's competitor intelligence beyond a surface scan. Repeat for each competitor on the research list.

## Step 1: Identify & Scope
- [ ] Confirm competitor name, website URL, and primary location
- [ ] Confirm they are a real local competitor (same service category + geography)
- [ ] Note any sub-brands or sister locations

## Step 2: Live Pricing
- [ ] Record per-service prices (massage, facials, body treatments, etc.)
- [ ] Note "starts at" / "from" rates vs. fixed prices
- [ ] Capture any dynamic or seasonal pricing (peak/off-peak, weekday/weekend)
- [ ] Flag services with no published price (price-on-request)

## Step 3: Packages & Memberships
- [ ] Bundles / multi-service packages and their pricing
- [ ] Membership tiers, monthly fees, and member perks
- [ ] Gift cards offered?
- [ ] Intro / first-visit offers and discounts

## Step 4: Booking-Flow Friction
- [ ] Identify the booking platform (Squarespace/Acuity/Vagaro/Mindbody/Boulevard/other)
- [ ] Count the number of steps from landing to confirmed booking
- [ ] Is an account required to book?
- [ ] Is a deposit or card-on-file required?
- [ ] Test mobile experience - does the flow work cleanly on a phone?

## Step 5: Reviews & Ratings
- [ ] Google: review count, average rating, recency of newest reviews
- [ ] Yelp (and any other relevant platform): review count + average rating
- [ ] Owner response cadence - do they reply to reviews, and how recently?
- [ ] Note recurring praise/complaint themes

## Step 6: GBP Completeness
- [ ] Claimed / verified?
- [ ] Primary + secondary categories
- [ ] Photo count and freshness
- [ ] Google Posts active?
- [ ] Q&A present and answered?
- [ ] Attributes set (amenities, accessibility, etc.)
- [ ] Hours accurate?
- [ ] Booking link present and working?

## Step 7: Paid Search Presence
- [ ] Search their brand name - do they bid on it?
- [ ] Search 3-5 category terms (e.g. "day spa atlantic city") - do they show ads?
- [ ] Capture ad copy themes / headlines
- [ ] Note landing page used (homepage vs. dedicated LP)

## Step 8: Record Sources & Capture Date
- [ ] Log a source URL for every data point above
- [ ] Stamp the capture date (data goes stale - this matters)
- [ ] Mark any inferred or unverified data points explicitly

## Step 9: Document & Summarize
- Write the competitor's section into 01_Clients/[Client]/SEO/Competitor Research.md
- Update Agent Memory under SEO Notes with key takeaways
- Note gaps/opportunities vs. the client (pricing edge, booking friction to beat, review deficit, paid-search openings)
- Append a line to 11_Agents/memory-log.md

## Tools Needed
- Live competitor websites + booking flows
- Google Search / SERP checks (incognito)
- Google Business Profile listings, Yelp
