# SOP: Facebook Ads Reporting

## When to Use
When generating client reports (weekly or monthly).

## Metrics to Pull
- Spend
- Impressions
- Reach
- Frequency
- Clicks (link clicks, not all clicks)
- CTR (link click-through rate)
- CPC (cost per link click)
- CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions)
- Results (leads, purchases, etc.)
- Cost per result
- ROAS (if e-commerce)

## Report Structure
1. **Summary** - Overall performance vs previous period
2. **Campaign Breakdown** - Per campaign metrics
3. **Top Performers** - Best ads and audiences
4. **Issues** - What's underperforming and why
5. **Recommendations** - What to do next
6. **Budget Recommendation** - Scale, maintain, or reduce

## Where to Save
- Update client's Reporting Log in Obsidian
- Send summary via Slack
- Log session

## Notes
- Always compare to previous period
- Lead with wins, then issues, then recommendations
- Keep it concise - clients don't read long reports
